DEV-1766 — AFTER fix (v17.1.0)

Fixed: Calling updateSettings({ colWidths: undefined }) on a table with nestedHeaders enabled now correctly re-expands columns to fit cell content.
Root cause fixed: AutoColumnSize now registers its modifyColWidth hook at priority -10 (before NestedHeaders at default priority 0). This ensures ACS-measured content widths are factored in before the nested-header ghost-table floor is applied.
Steps to verify:
  1. Table initialises with colWidths: 100 and nestedHeaders.
  2. Click Reset colWidths to call updateSettings({ colWidths: undefined }).
  3. Observe column 0 — it now expands to fit the long content (FIXED ✅).
