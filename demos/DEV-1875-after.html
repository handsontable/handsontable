New visibleWhen option:
Each header in nestedHeaders can declare visibleWhen: 'collapsed' | 'expanded' | 'always':
  • 'expanded' — visible only while the group is expanded (Jan, Feb, Mar)
  • 'collapsed' — visible only while the group is collapsed (Total summary)
  • 'always' — visible in both states
Try it: Click the collapse button (▲) on the "Q1 2025" header. Jan, Feb, Mar hide and Total appears. Expand again to restore the detail columns.
