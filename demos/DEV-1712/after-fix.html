✅ FIX DEMO v17.1.0 — AFTER FIX DEV-1712 (PR #12594)
Fixed: The comment editor is now always clamped inside the browser viewport. Even on narrow portrait screens the comment stays fully visible — it no longer flies off to the left.
How to verify the fix:
  1. Open this page in Chrome DevTools → mobile emulation (iPhone 12 Pro / Pixel 5, portrait — ~390 px wide).
  2. Click on cell B2 or E3 (cells with a red triangle in the corner).
  3. Observe: the comment popup now appears inside the viewport, fully visible.
💡 Tip: You can also resize your browser window to be very narrow (~400 px) to see the fix on desktop.

What changed (PR #12594)

Two new pure helpers in comments/viewport.ts:

• shrinkSizeToViewport() — caps the editor's display width/height to innerWidth/Height − scrollbarWidth − 2×8px. Persisted comment.style values are untouched.

• clampPositionToViewport() — after the existing flip logic, clamps the final x/y so the editor never leaves the visible area regardless of scroll position or viewport size.
