Bug: On narrow (portrait) mobile viewports, the comment editor appears to the left of the commented cell — flying off-screen and becoming invisible. The flip logic moved the editor left to avoid right-edge overflow, but never checked that the flipped position itself stayed inside the viewport.
How to reproduce:
  1. Open this page in Chrome DevTools → mobile emulation (iPhone 12 Pro / Pixel 5, portrait — ~390 px wide).
  2. Click on cell B2 (the cell with a red triangle in the corner).
  3. Observe: the comment popup appears off-screen to the left — invisible.
💡 Tip: You can also resize your browser window to be very narrow (~400 px) to see the effect on desktop.
