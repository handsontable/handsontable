DEV-1723 — Filter by value of column Y is lost after editing column X

Version: v17.0.1 BUG v17.1.0 FIXED
Steps to reproduce the bug:
1. Click the ▾ dropdown on the Country header.
2. Go to Filter by value → uncheck France → click OK.
3. Click the ▾ dropdown on the Car header.
4. Go to Filter by value → uncheck BMW → click OK.
5. Double-click any cell in the Country column, press Enter (no change needed).
6. Click the ▾ dropdown on the Car header → open Filter by value again.
⚠ Bug: All checkboxes in the Car filter are now unchecked. The "BMW excluded" selection was silently wiped by editing a cell in Country column.
