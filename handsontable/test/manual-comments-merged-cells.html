Comments on merged cells — manual test

This page verifies that the comment editor appears flush with the right (or left in RTL) edge of a merged cell. The anchor width is derived from the cell meta colspan, not from the DOM element's colSpan attribute.

LTR — comments + merged cells

How to test:
  1. Right-click on the merged cell A1:C1 and choose Add/Edit comment.
  2. The editor should open immediately to the right of the merged cell (not just to the right of column A).
  3. Right-click on a regular (non-merged) cell, e.g. D2, and add a comment.
  4. The editor should open to the right of that single cell.
  5. Scroll the table to the right so merged cell partially goes offscreen, then open the comment. The editor should flip to the left of the cell.

RTL — comments + merged cells

How to test:
  1. Right-click on the merged cell A1:C1 and choose Add/Edit comment.
  2. The editor should open to the left of the merged cell's left edge.
