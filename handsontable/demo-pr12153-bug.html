PR #12153 - Mixed checkbox multi-selection Delete / Undo

Build: Released v17.0.1 BUG PR #12153 Fix FIXED
BUG present (v17.0.1). When a multi-layer selection (Ctrl+A then Ctrl+click inner range) is deleted with the Delete key, checkbox cells may receive a noValue CSS class (broken visual state), multiple undo actions may be registered instead of one, and Ctrl+Z may fail to restore all original data.
How to observe the bug:
  1. Click "Select All + inner range + Delete".
  2. BUG: Undo action count may be > 1. Checkboxes may show noValue class.
  3. Click "Undo".
  4. BUG: Data may not be fully restored — some cells remain null.
  5. Compare with the Fix page.
Undo actions after Delete
—
Expected: 1 — BUG: may be > 1
Checkbox "noValue" count
—
Expected: 0 — BUG: may be > 0
Data restored after Undo?
—
Expected: YES — BUG: may be NO
Click "Select All + inner range + Delete" to test.
